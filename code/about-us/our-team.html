Wesley Bor

Managing Director

Wesley Bor holds a PhD, Master and Bachelor of Science degree in Food, Nutrition and Dietetics and has over 14 years’ experience in public, private sector and non-governmental organization. Wesley has served in Kabarak University for over 4 years. Prior to joining Kabarak University as founding head of department for Human Nutrition and Public Health, Wesley Coordinated Nutrition Program for the Presidential Emergency Fund for Aids Relief (PEPFAR) Walter Reed DOD project in South Rift Valley for a period of 6 years.

Treasurer
