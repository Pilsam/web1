Our Team

Mercy Corps' global team of over 5,600 humanitarians work in more than 40 countries around the world. We share a steadfast belief that communities are the best agents of their own change, with 9 out of 10 of our team members from the countries where they work.

Executive Committee

Wesley Bor

Managing Director

Wesley Bor holds a PhD, Master and Bachelor of Science degree in Food, Nutrition and Dietetics and has over 14 years’ experience in public, private sector and non-governmental organization. Wesley has served in Kabarak University for over 4 years. Prior to joining Kabarak University as founding head of department for Human Nutrition and Public Health, Wesley Coordinated Nutrition Program for the Presidential Emergency Fund for Aids Relief (PEPFAR) Walter Reed DOD project in South Rift Valley for a period of 6 years.

Dr. Pamela Wadende

Treasurer
Dr Wadende is a developmental Psychologist with qualifications that entrench her in the field of pedagogy and human behaviour. She is a senior lecturer, and Post Graduate studies coordinator at Kisii University’s Kericho campus.
