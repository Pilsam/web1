About KYC

Konnect Youth Consortium (KYC) is an independent, non-profit, non-governmental organization, with its headquarters (HQ) located in Kericho, Kenya and registered with the Kenya NGO coordination bureau since June 2007. The organizationâ€™s has other branches in Narok, Bomet and Kilgoris and is at present carrying out successful programs targeting the young people.

These programs include Health: with a focus on HIV/AIDS Prevention, provision of Comprehensive Reproductive Health Services. Youth empowerment programs: these include leadership development, creative arts, peace building and social cohesion. Environmental conservation: KYC has supported initiatives and measures to respond to key environmental issues and challenges and emerging global environmental concerns.
